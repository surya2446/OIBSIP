"Missile Man of India"

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam

15 October 1931 – 27 July 2015

Biography

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam,(born October 15,1931,Rameswaram,India—died July 27,2015,Shillong),Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India’s missile and nuclear weapons programs. He was president of India from 2002 to 2007.Kalam earned a degree in aeronautical engineering from the Madras Institute of Technology and in 1958 joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
